The following is a set of quotes that I’m drawn to for one reason or another. It is not exhaustive, nor is it well documented. Read on if you’re curious, maybe a quote will speak to you like it spoke to me.

Another note … These quotes are in no particular order and no particular categorization. They just spoke to me at one point or the other. I should attribute and apologies for not doing so. Reach out to me if you want the origin of one and I’ll see what I can do [although a google search should take you pretty far]. This list will be updated over time.

The most important thing to do when you find yourself in a hole is to stop digging. - Buffett

Optionality is the property of asymmetric upside (preferably unlimited) with correspondingly limited downside (preferably tiny) - Taleb, Antifragile

The future is already here – it’s just not evenly distributed.

Amateurs talked strategy, professionals study logistics. - Omar Bradley

Organizations, like people, have values. To be effective in an organization, a person’s values must be compatible with the organization’s values. They do not need to be the same, but they must be close enough to coexist. Otherwise, the person will not only be frustrated but also will not produce results. - Drucker

Inspiration is for amateurs. The rest of us just show up and get to work. - Chuck Close

The will to win is not nearly so important as the will to prepare to win. - Vince Lombardi

Conway’s law. Organizations which design systems … are constrained to produce designs which are copies of the communication structures of these organizations — M. Conway

Life is not a journey to the grave with the intention of arriving safely in a pretty, well-preserved body. But rather, to skid in broadside, thoroughly used up, totally worn out, and loudly proclaiming… WOW what a ride!!!

One my favorite quotes from Maya Angelou is “when someone shows you who they are, believe them the first time.” If you get nothing else from this article, get this. You cannot change another person. Here, I’ll say it again, you cannot change another person.

3 Rules for Decision Making Under Crisis

  • You never get blamed for what you did wrong.
  • You always get blamed for how you tried to cover up what you did wrong.
  • You never remember situations 1 and 2.

Doubt is only removed by action, if you`re not working then that’s when doubt comes in.

To get something you’ve never had, you have to do something you never did.

Look forward to the past. - Pavel Tsatsouline

Stop listening to yourself, start talking to yourself.

Don’t get bitter, get better.

Most men live lives of quiet desperation.

You don’t get what you want, you get what you need.

When someone shows you who they are, believe them the first time. - Maya Angelou

To work in an organization whose value system is unacceptable or incompatible with one’s own condemns a person both to frustration and to nonperformance.

Idle hands are the devil’s workshop.

Hard times create strong men. Strong men create good times. Good times create weak men. Weak men create hard times.
